Dzenn is a small **innovation lab** I run in my spare time — a place where I experiment with ideas, build weird little tools, and ship products I personally find interesting.

The world feels a bit unpredictable lately. Prices keep rising, technology moves fast, conflicts appear in different parts of the world, and the future often feels uncertain. Instead of worrying too much about it, I decided to do something simple:

**build things while I still can.**

Dzenn is my space to explore ideas without overthinking them. Some projects become useful tools, some turn into experiments, and some simply teach me something new.

As the saying goes:

> **Code while coding is still allowed.**

## What I've Built So Far

Some of the projects currently built under Dzenn include:

<HoverLinkPreview href="https://github.com/dzenn-app/pomodoro" previewImage="https://res.cloudinary.com/dctl5pihh/video/upload/v1773037353/dzenn-final_ycbwue.mov"><strong>Dzenn Pomodoro</strong></HoverLinkPreview>  
A small **floating timer for macOS** designed to stay visible without getting in the way. It helps maintain focus while working, with a lightweight and distraction-free interface.

<HoverLinkPreview href="https://dzenn.live/" previewImage="https://res.cloudinary.com/dctl5pihh/image/upload/v1775959409/Screenshot_2026-04-12_at_09.03.07_mgzxuj.png"><strong>Dzenn Link</strong></HoverLinkPreview>  
A **nonchalant link-in-bio platform** focused on design flexibility and aesthetics. Instead of rigid templates, it allows creators to present their links in a more expressive and customizable layout.

More experiments and tools will continue to appear here as ideas come and go.
